What is more interesting than looking for a treasure with your best friend?

Be prepared to handle the cuteness of Roojack world. Help Maple and Whippy to prove that one of the urban legends from Roojack was right. Legend says that a treasure was hidden somewhere. In that treasure, there exists something so powerful that can conquer the world.

Maple and Whippy were raised hearing that story, now they're ready to start an adventure to find the treasure. Help them to fulfill their destiny, find the treasure, then conquer the Roojack world together (of course if the legend is real).
